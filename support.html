Getting Started

SkinRoutine helps you build a skincare habit, finish the products you buy, and see your progress over time.

  • Create a routine: Tap the + button on the Today tab to add a routine and the products you use.
  • Check in daily: Tap the circle next to each routine to log your skincare.
  • Track empty bottles: Long-press a product pot in My Garden to mark it empty.
  • Set reminders: Open a routine and turn on reminders to get a gentle nudge.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data stored in the cloud?
No. All your data is stored locally on your iPhone. No account is required.

Can I export my data?
Data export is not supported yet, but it is on our roadmap.

How do I delete a routine?
Open a routine, scroll down, and tap "Archive Routine." You can restore archived routines from Settings.
